About Us

Welcome to Trend Blendz Barbershop, where exceptional haircuts and unparalleled service meet. Located in the heart of Lakewood, CO, we take pride in delivering the finest grooming experiences for men, teens, and kids alike. Our team of skilled barbers is dedicated to perfecting the art of barbering. Led by Fabian, a passionate barber who discovered his talent and love for the craft at a young age, Trend Blendz Barbershop is the culmination of his journey. Starting as a hobby at the age of 14, Fabian's natural knack for barbering flourished over the years, eventually leading him to transform his passion into a rewarding career.
With years of experience working in various barbershops after high school, Fabian honed his skills and acquired the expertise necessary to create flawless fades, intricate designs, and precise straight razor eyebrows. His unwavering commitment to quality and attention to detail set the foundation for Trend Blendz.
